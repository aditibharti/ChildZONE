1 Raju, Shalu and kalu

Raju is Shalu's elder brother. They have a dog.They call it Kalu. The three of them like to play together.

They go to a nearby pond along with their uncle. Raju and Shalu play in water. Their uncle sits on a rock. "Do not go in deep water," says uncle.

The children call,"Come in kalu. It's fun in water. Come, come, come!"

Kalu Come in. It like water."It's fun in water," says Raju.

"I have a ball," says Shalu. "Here is the ball,Kalu," say Shalu. She throws the ball. "Get the ball Kalu," says Raju. Kalu runs for the ball. Kalu likes to have fun. Shalu and Raju like to have fum. they all enjoy fun in water.
